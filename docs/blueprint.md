# **App Name**: Arlo Bistro

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Dinner Menu Display: Showcase the complete dinner menu with categories, item descriptions, and pricing, allowing for easy updates by restaurant staff.
- Online Reservation System: Allow diners to conveniently book a table for dinner service, including selection of date, time, and party size.
- Private Events Inquiry Form: A dedicated contact form for guests to inquire about and request bookings for private brunch events.
- Restaurant Information & Contact: Clearly display the restaurant's address, operating hours, phone number, and a map integration for easy navigation.
- AI Chef's Recommendation Tool: An AI tool that suggests a 'Chef's Daily Special' or wine pairing based on menu items, seasonality, or common French culinary themes, providing a personalized touch.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, muted grey-blue (#5A6672) to convey a refined, classic French bistro atmosphere.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated grey-blue (#EFF1F3) providing a clean and airy canvas for content.
- Accent color: A soft, refreshing light blue-green (#98C9D7) for subtle highlights and interactive elements, analogous to the primary while offering visual interest.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif), chosen for its elegant, high-contrast, and sophisticated feel, perfect for a refined French-inspired establishment.
- Body text font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif), selected for its modern readability and warmth, ensuring comfortable legibility for menu descriptions and general information.
- Utilize minimalistic, line-art style icons that complement the elegant and clean design, specifically for navigation, reservations, and contact information.
- Implement a clean and spacious layout with ample whitespace to evoke a sense of refinement and luxury, focusing on clear hierarchy for menus and important information.
